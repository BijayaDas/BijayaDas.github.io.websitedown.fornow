About Us:-
DockYard is a digital product agency offering bespoke software, mobile, and web application development consulting. The company provides exceptional services in strategy, user experience, design, and full stack engineering to bring brilliant digital ideas into being for innovative clients such as Netflix, Apple, Zipcar, McGraw-Hill, Nasdaq, Harvard, Fidelity Investments, Constant Contact, CollegeVine, and WNYC, as well as funded startups. DockYard helps organizations transform ambitious, complex ideas into fully deployed apps using Ember.js, Elixir, and Phoenix. DockYard translates business needs into industry-leading solutions that advance organizational goals and enhance user experiences. The digital products they create for clients establish high benchmarks for engagement, efficiency, and elegance. DockYard integrated approach allows them to solve problems quickly with stability, maintainability, and scalability. The company is smart, experienced, and deeply knowledgeable about modern application UX, design, and architecture. DockYard contributes to — if not leads — the open source technologies of the future, including Elixir, Phoenix, and LiveView. The company also offers testing and QA, project management, training, support, and staff augmentation. And with consultants nationwide, they have staff in key markets across the United States.
